About ClickSafe

In today's digital landscape, app stores and online content are filled with misleading applications and deceptive headlines designed to manipulate users.

Our Fraud App Detection Model helps identify suspicious apps by analyzing user reviews, sentiment, and app details like ratings and installs. It detects patterns that indicate fraud, helping users avoid unsafe or misleading applications.

Our Clickbait Detection Model scans headlines to spot misleading or exaggerated content using natural language processing (NLP). By analyzing wording and emotional triggers, it helps users avoid deceptive articles and clickbait traps. Together, these models create a safer and more transparent online experience.
